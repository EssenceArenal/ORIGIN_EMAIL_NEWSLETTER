What is Origin

Everyone, Everywhere, Together.

ORIGIN is an unprecedented worldwide event bringing together ancestral wisdom, modern innovation, and collective participation to reconnect humanity with its shared roots and shared responsibility.

From family living rooms to city parks and natural landscapes — everyone is invited. The climax is a synchronized Moment of Global Unity when millions pause together to affirm a positive vision for a new Earth.
