What is Origin

Everyone,
Everywhere,
Together.

ORIGIN is an unprecedented worldwide event — in-person and online — bringing together ancestral wisdom, modern innovation, and collective participation to reconnect humanity with its shared roots and shared responsibility.

From family living rooms and backyards to public gatherings in clubs, community centers, city parks, and natural landscapes — everyone is invited to participate. The climax is a synchronized Moment of Global Unity, when millions of people around the world pause together to affirm a positive vision for a new Earth.

First Nations Elders

The Invocation
for a New Earth

A flowing prayer composed of lines contributed by Indigenous Elders from many Nations. Each Elder contributes words drawn from a traditional prayer, song, or teaching — spoken first in their own language, then in English. The prayer begins in Aotearoa New Zealand, the first land to meet the sun, and travels across the world as a symbolic wave of intention circling the planet.
